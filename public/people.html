The Santa Cruz Planning Department

Please know you are always welcome to visit the Planning Department during their open hours: Mon to Thurs 7:30am-12pm.

The Planning Department’s public counter is located in room 206 of City Hall, 809 Center Street, Santa Cruz, CA. It’s on the second floor in the annex building.

Open Monday – Thursday • 7:30 am to 12 pm Closed Fridays.

Staff will be available in the afternoon by phone or appointment only. The office is closed from 12-1 for lunch, 5pm is quitting time.

Contact planningcounter@cityofsantacruz.com Tel: 831-420-5100 • Fax: 831-420-5434
