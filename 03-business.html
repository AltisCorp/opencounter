Narrative about the different types of businesses you can start. There are business where you make something (manufacturing), where you sell something (retail) where you provide a service (service) where you feed people. It's important to know what type of business you have, because that'll impact what regulations and guidelines apply to you. Starting a business requires some paperwork. The paperwork is dependent on what you do and where you are located. Every business in Santa Cruz needs a business license and a use permit, even if you are working from home. If you are working from home, you can apply online for your business license and use permit. Click here to apply. If your business is in a space other than your home, it is more complicated.
